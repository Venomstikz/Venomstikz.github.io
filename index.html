Explosive Bunnies

Posted on the first day of the apocalypse(it's already started)

Bunnies have adapted strangely to some of the recent nuclear explosions. The former populated area known as California,(now destroyed by nuclear war)home to many domestic rabbits. Is now a wasteland of it's former self. The streets are deserted with wreckage and debris everywhere.These rabbits however, survived the blast and suprisingly, the fallout. They somehow have absorbed the nuclear energy and have escaped the wrecage untouched. They now roam the areas near California with a strange ability. THEY EXPLODE. If you come into contact with one, run away immediately. Alert a local official and they will send in a team to exterminate it safely.
